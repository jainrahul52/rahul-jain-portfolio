Research Statement

Computer Vision • Deep Learning • Temporal Video Analysis • HCI

My research focuses on creating robust deep learning systems for dynamic hand gesture recognition and temporal video analysis. I design architectures that combine spatial CNN backbones with temporal modeling (LSTM / attention / transformers), encoded motion representations, and ensemble techniques for high accuracy on real-world video datasets.

Ph.D. Thesis

Dynamic Hand Gesture Recognition Using Deep Learning Based Approaches — Ph.D. thesis (NIT Silchar, 2024). The thesis introduces encoded motion image techniques, two-level detection-classification frameworks and ensemble strategies to improve recognition on video streams.
